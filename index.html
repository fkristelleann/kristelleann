Meet the chairman of Intalio - the ambitious company looking to develop its business into the UK

The new group focuses on providing solutions for the healthcare, education, finance and construction sectors

  • Share
  •  
By
Olivia Rose Fox
  • 08:56, 23 SEP 2020
Advertorial

Everteam Global Services, the content services provider and software vendor based in France, has joined forces with Intalio, the leader in intelligent business process management to form the Intalio group.

The resulting union, keeping the name of Intalio, has accelerated the international development of both companies, enabling them to strengthen their presence in key sectors; including in the highly competitive SME market in Europe, Central Asia and North America.

At the heart of this sustainable expansion strategy is a dedicated group of experts led by the CEO of the new group, Antoine Hraoui.

We spoke to Antoine Hraoui, Chairman and CEO of the group, to discuss the union and the strategic plans for 2020/2021.

Antoine Hraoui - The CEO with four decades of experience

Antoine originally kick-started his career within large companies based in the USA and France, thus acquiring a valuable experience with a successful track record in managing and developing IT companies.

Antoine then moved on from multinational companies and dedicated his efforts to create and launch several startups that he subsequently converted into multinational companies.

His latest major investment was in Everteam Global Services, which he was able to transform from a French IT SME into a leading multinational IT group.

Fast forward a little and this year, Antoine has made the decision to join forces with Intalio in order to create a major provider of content services, process management and data governance solutions.

Antoines forward-thinking mindset and professional endeavours have allowed him to build an impressive repertoire of true business successes, globally.

What does the union mean for you?

Antoine said: This union truly responds to the needs of our clients as we continually develop our service offerings.

In an ever-changing economic situation and a hyper-competitive sector, digital transformation is a key component to allow enterprises to maintain their competitive advantage.

Everteam Global Services (the French provider of content services) and Intalio (the US provider of iBPMS) are combining their strengths to focus on smart acquisitions. This allows us to attain a larger market share and ensure a wider reach across different sectors and regions.

It also introduced tremendous opportunities by pushing geographical and structural development further.

Subsequently, Intalio now offers a comprehensive product portfolio and a robust micro-service offering not only for content management, but also process automation and data governance".

He added: To further adapt to this advancement, Intalio places emphasis on strengthening its positioning and investments in data governance and analytics.

Our R&D team will continue to leverage Machine Learning, Automatic Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Cognitive Services to collect and analyse unstructured data.

"This combination allows us to increase customer satisfaction through a diverse portfolio of industry specific solutions, while gaining a broader reach and a larger market share".

What will the new identity mean for existing customers?

Strategy is at the heart of this sustainable expansion as a dedicated team of engineers and board members that specialise in a variety of essential industries such as healthcare, education, finance and construction collaborate together.

With the combined talent and expertise of this workforce, Intalio continues to offer cutting-edge solutions in the ever-evolving domain of digital transformation which has become a cornerstone for organisations around the world.

We continue to provide a comprehensive list of solutions that extend beyond advanced content management to include process automation and data governance applied into industry specific solutions".

About Intalio

Intalio is an international software provider with 30 years of experience in innovation within the field of content and process services.

By combining cutting-edge technologies with AI and machine learning to introduce advanced business solutions, Intalio strives to be a trusted leader in digital transformation across the US, Europe, and further afield.

Our unique micro-services architecture includes a content-centric application building solution that focuses on business workflow to improve productivity and efficiency.

This new identity will become a major player in the industry, especially in the top European markets such as the UK and the United States.

Our customers needs remain a priority to us as we continue business at a heightened level of excellence and professionalism.

Our existing customers leverage a renowned product portfolio whilst adopting the latest technologies and flexibility to adhere to their specific business needs."

Can you tell me more about your new geographic expansion?

Our headquarters remain in France as we attain a considerable growth in Europe by opening offices and recruiting partners in new European countries.

"We plan to develop our businesses further in Europe through a set of renowned and industry specific solutions.

"We are executing a strategic and determined business development plan in the UK as early as autumn of this year.

"Germany, Belgium, Switzerland, Poland and Norway are also targeted for the remainder of 2020 and 2021".
